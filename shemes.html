Government Schemes for Farmers

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)

A central government scheme that provides direct financial assistance to farmers in the form of Rs. 6,000 per year, paid in three installments.

Read More

Krishi Sahayog Yojana (Agricultural Assistance Scheme)

A state government initiative to provide financial aid to farmers for the purchase of agricultural equipment, seeds, fertilizers, and other inputs to improve farm productivity.

Read More

Goa State Land Development Corporation (GSLDC)

GSLDC offers various schemes for the development of land and improving agricultural practices, including subsidized loans and assistance for irrigation systems.

Read More

National Horticulture Mission (NHM)

A central government initiative aimed at promoting the development of horticultural crops, providing financial assistance for the establishment of new orchards, irrigation systems, and post-harvest management.

Read More

Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY)

A scheme for agricultural development that supports the creation of infrastructure, improving productivity, and modernizing agricultural practices with a focus on sustainability

Read More

Soil Health Management Scheme

This scheme is designed to provide financial assistance for soil health management, including soil testing, organic farming, and nutrient management to ensure soil fertility and better crop yields.

Read More

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

A crop insurance scheme under the central government that provides financial protection to farmers against crop loss due to natural calamities, pests, and diseases.

Read More

Sub-Mission on Agricultural Mechanization (SMAM)

This scheme provides financial assistance for the purchase of agricultural machinery like tractors, harvesters, and other farm equipment to promote mechanization and reduce labor costs.

Read More

Subsidy for Greenhouse and Polyhouse Farming

The government provides subsidies for the construction of greenhouses and polyhouses to promote controlled-environment farming, which can enhance productivity and extend the growing season.

Read More

Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana (PMKSY)

PMKSY focuses on the efficient use of water for agricultural purposes and aims to achieve perennial irrigation for every farm.

Read More

Pradhan Mantri Annadata Aay Sanrakshan Abhiyan (PM-AASHA)

PM-AASHA seeks to protect farmers' incomes from price fluctuations in the market, which often lead to exploitation and low returns for agricultural produce.
